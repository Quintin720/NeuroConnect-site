Hi, I’m the founder of NeuroConnect

I was diagnosed as Autistic in my mid-forties. That moment didn’t come with answers, it came with a flood of questions, grief, relief, and realisation. Looking back, I saw a lifetime of masking, misunderstanding, and trying to survive in a world that wasn’t designed with me in mind.

NeuroConnect was born from that realisation. It’s not just a website, it’s a place I wish I had when I was first navigating my diagnosis. A space that’s honest, empowering, and built on lived experience. I created NeuroConnect to make the journey gentler for others like me, and for those who support us.

What I’m Building

This platform is designed to connect, inform, and uplift. Through NeuroConnect, I want to help people understand autism from an Autistic perspective, not just a textbook. That means:

  • Creating neuroaffirmative training for workplaces and educators
  • Offering AI support that’s actually informed by Autistic lived experience
  • Writing from the heart through my blog

What I Believe

Being Autistic isn’t a problem to solve. It’s a different way of experiencing the world. When people are supported, understood, and accepted, they thrive. NeuroConnect is here to help make that happen; one conversation, one connection at a time.
